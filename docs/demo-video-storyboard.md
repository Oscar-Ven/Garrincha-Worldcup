## Recording Notes

- Capture desktop at 1920x1080 for the main video.
- Capture mobile at 390x844 or 430x932 for vertical insert shots.
- Keep browser zoom at 100 percent.
- Hide bookmarks and unnecessary browser UI.
- Use preview data only; do not enter real users, secrets, or client data.

## Asset Notes

- Current app uses placeholder GARRINCHA wordmark styling, placeholder QR card, and placeholder prize copy.
- Do not copy client PDF or website imagery into the app unless the client approves the assets for this project.
- Replace placeholders after receiving official GARRINCHA/TIFO campaign artwork.
