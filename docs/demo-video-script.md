This package is for a professional explainer video for the GARRINCHA World Cup Prediction App. Supabase live testing is postponed; the video should use preview/demo data only.

### Scene-By-Scene Script

1. Campaign entry, 0-6s
   - Visual: GARRINCHA campaign hero with QR placeholder.
   - Voiceover: "The World Cup becomes a center-wide challenge with the GARRINCHA prediction app."
   - On-screen text: "Scan. Register. Predict."

2. Registration, 6-12s
   - Visual: Registration fields and center selection.
   - Voiceover: "Players scan a QR code, register in minutes, and choose their GARRINCHA Center."

3. Predict matches, 12-23s

   - Voiceover: "They predict World Cup scores, edit before kickoff, and keep every match easy to understand."
   - On-screen text: "Predict until kickoff."

4. Lock and score, 23-32s
   - Visual: Locked match and admin final score entry.
   - Voiceover: "At kickoff, predictions lock. After full time, admins enter the official score."

5. Points and ranking, 32-41s
   - Visual: Points cards and leaderboards.
   - Voiceover: "Points calculate automatically, bonus points can be awarded with a reason, and players climb global, national, and center leaderboards."
   - On-screen text: "Global. National. Center."

6. CTA, 41-45s
   - Visual: Final call-to-action.
   - Voiceover: "Bring the World Cup campaign to every GARRINCHA Center."
   - On-screen text: "GARRINCHA World Cup Prediction App"

## Suggested Music Style

- Upbeat football campaign track.
- Percussive but clean, not too aggressive.
- Tempo around 110-125 BPM.
- Avoid vocals so the voiceover stays clear.

## Suggested Transition Style

- Fast but simple cuts between sections.
- Light zoom on QR, prediction inputs, and leaderboard rows.
- Use short slide-up transitions for mobile captures.
- Keep transitions consistent with the campaign feel; avoid flashy effects.

## Ending Call-To-Action

Primary ending text:

"Bring the World Cup prediction campaign to every GARRINCHA Center."

Secondary ending text:

"Add official banners, prize copy, QR code, and launch with live Supabase data."
